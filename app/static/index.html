Minimum Inhibitory Concentration Predictor

Submit a peptide sequence and a target organism. The model estimates the MIC needed to inhibit visible growth of that strain.

Bachelor's degree project Trained on DBAASP data Research use only

This tool is a bachelor's degree project by Zaza Elizbarashvili and Nika Macharadze. The underlying model is trained on antimicrobial peptide activity data from DBAASP (Database of Antimicrobial Activity and Structure of Peptides). It is intended for research and educational purposes only — not for clinical, diagnostic, or therapeutic use.
